ACalendar - Event Planning Made Easy!

ACalendar is an Android calendar app that helps users to more easily manage their events and schedules, and to create and share events. With ACalendar, you can easily reschedule events to another time, invite others to your events, or find events near you that you are interested in!

More info about ACalendar can be found in our product description.
